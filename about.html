ABOUT "TAH-NYA YOO-SEF"

"Born in Indonesia and raised in New York City, life without art and design seems unbearable to me. I grew up in QUeens and currently atten Queens College, majoring in Gprahic Design and minoring in Interactive Design, to pursue my drea. I believe that a good designer should not establish their style, but also be able to alter it for their client.

My art reflects on the diversity I was immersed in growing up. Things in life can be busy and extravagant, or simple and sleek in both Indonesia and New York City. The tropics of my hometown and city life of where I currently reside plays a big part in the rpocess of my works.

Still fresh in the field, I've worked with clients in the entertainment industry to create event flyers for events. I hope to broaden my horizon in gaining knowledge and experiencing work in further industry."
